How to Play Blackjack

1. This is a game between player and dealer.

2. In this 2 to 10 cards has their own value like card 2 has value 2.

3. In this game A has two values 1 and 11. The value will depend on your score.

4. J,Q,K has the value same value that is 10.

5. Here you need to score as near to 21 as you can.But if your score will get higher than 21 than you will be bust means you will lose the game.

6. You can press the hit button and it will generate random card that card's value will be added to the score.

7. you can press hit button as many time as you want.once you feel like this is the nearest value from 21 and you want to use it as your score, press stand button.

8. When you will press button, the dealer will generate his cards and try to get higher score than yours.

9. if dealer gets bust then you will win and if dealer gets score which is higher than yours and below or equal to 21 then you will lose.

10. as an example suppose you press stand at score 20 and dealer gets the score 21 than you will lose.
